Malaysian Cultures

Malaysia's is a vibrant mix of Malay, Chinese, and Indian influences, reflecting the country's diverse population. The majority of Malaysians identify as Malay, with many practicing Islam and Malay traditions, while also speaking Bahasa Malaysia, a language rooted in Austronesian origins.

Malay Cultures

Islam

Dominant religion among the Malay population, significantly influencing customs and traditions.

Bahasa Malaysia

National language, reflecting rich linguistic heritage of the region.

Traditional Practices

Dances, music and art forms like batik, deeply rooted in Malay culture.

Festivals

Celebrations like Hari Raya Aidilfitri and Hari Raya Aidiladha, major Islamic holidays are celebrated throughout the country.
